---

## Risk Categories

### High Risk
Emails attempting credential theft, financial fraud, or unauthorized access.

### Medium Risk
Emails containing suspicious behavior requiring additional verification.

### Low Risk
Legitimate or verified communications without malicious indicators.

---

## Conclusion

Proper phishing classification helps organizations prioritize incident response and improve employee awareness against social engineering attacks.
