URL & Domain Analysis

Objective

The purpose of this analysis was to inspect suspicious URLs identified within phishing email samples.

⸻

Indicators Identified

1. Fake Domain Names

Several phishing emails used domains closely resembling trusted organizations.

Examples:

* microsoft-account-verification[.]com
* paypal-security-update[.]com

⸻

2. Insecure HTTP Usage

Multiple suspicious links used HTTP instead of secure HTTPS connections.

⸻

3. Credential Harvesting Pages

Links attempted to redirect users to fake login or verification pages designed to steal credentials.

⸻

4. Social Engineering Through URLs

Domains used words such as:

* verify
* secure
* authentication
* update

to appear trustworthy.

⸻

Recommendation

Users should carefully inspect URLs before entering sensitive information and avoid interacting with suspicious domains.

⸻

Conclusion

The analyzed URLs demonstrated common phishing techniques including impersonation, fake verification pages, and misleading domain naming strategies.
