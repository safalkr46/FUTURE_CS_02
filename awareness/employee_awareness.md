# **Employee Awareness Guide**

## **What is Phishing?**

**Phishing** is a cyber attack technique where attackers impersonate trusted organizations or individuals to steal sensitive information such as **passwords**, **banking details**, or **OTPs**.

These attacks are commonly delivered through emails containing **fake links**, **urgent warnings**, or **malicious attachments**.

---

## **Common Targets**

- **Employees**
- **Banking customers**
- **Students**
- **Corporate organizations**
- **Remote workers**

---

## **Safe Email Practices**

- **Verify sender email addresses carefully**
- **Avoid opening suspicious links or attachments**
- **Never share passwords through email**
- **Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)**
- **Report suspicious emails immediately**

---

## **Importance of Security Awareness**

**Employee awareness** plays a major role in reducing **phishing-related security incidents** within organizations.
