Common Phishing Red Flags

Indicators Identified During Analysis

1. Urgent Language

Examples included:

* “Immediate action required”
* “Account suspension warning”

2. Suspicious Links

Several emails used domains attempting to imitate trusted companies.

3. Generic Greetings

Examples:

* Dear User
* Dear Customer

4. Fear & Pressure Tactics

Attackers attempted to create panic to force immediate responses.

5. Financial Requests

Business email compromise samples requested urgent money transfers.

⸻

Recommendation

Users should always verify sender identity and inspect links carefully before interacting with email content.
